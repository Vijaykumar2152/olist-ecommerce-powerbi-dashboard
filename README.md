# Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Sales & Customer Review Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyses the Olist Brazilian E-commerce dataset using Power BI to understand sales performance, customer behaviour, payment methods, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyse sales performance
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Understand customer payment preferences
- Measure delivery performance
- Analyse customer reviews and satisfaction

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modelling

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### Executive Summary
Provides an overview of:
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Total Items Sold
- Average Delivery Days
- Product Categories
- Payment Methods
- Monthly Sales Trend

### Sales Analysis
Provides insights into:
- Revenue by State
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- On-Time Deliveries
- Reviewed Orders
- Payment Value

### Customer Reviews
Provides insights into:
- Average Review Score
- Review Score Distribution
- Delivery Status vs Review Score
- Monthly Customer Satisfaction Trend

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Total Revenue exceeded **15.8M**
- Approximately **93%** of orders were delivered on time.
- Customers whose orders arrived on time gave significantly higher review scores.
- Credit Card was the most frequently used payment method.
- Sales were concentrated in São Paulo (SP).
- Customer satisfaction remained consistently above 4.0 throughout most of the year.
